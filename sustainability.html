Climate Fresk

At the beginning of my course at Enseeiht, I was given the opportunity to take part in a climate fresk. I worked with a team of other students and we discussed the different causes and consequences of climate change. We worked together to build a logical flow of information starting with human activities. Many of the cards we were given presented information that was enlightening and explained some consequences and links that we did not know about and had never heard about. After that activity, we took some time to reflect on it and think about what we could do ourselves to try and help reduce global warming and limit climate change as much as possible.
